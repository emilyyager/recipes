Mexican Corn in a Cup (Elote con Crema)

Source: Here

Ingredients

  • Canned, frozen or fresh whole kernel corn
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Chile
  • Lime
  • Mozzarella cheese (cotija cheese if you can find it)
  • Mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Split one can of corn into two microwave-safe cups.
  2. Put a pad of butter in each cup.
  3. Microwave for one minute. Stir the melted butter into the corn.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and chile. Squirt with lime juice. Add shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Add 2 tablespoons? Of mayonnaise per cup. Stir in all ingredients and serve
