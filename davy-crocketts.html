Davy Crocketts

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • onion
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Form ground beef into hamburger patties.
  2. Add onion.
  3. Spoon soup over meat.
  4. Add carrots and potatoes.
  5. Microwave for 20 minutes on medium.
