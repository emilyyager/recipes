Tabouleh

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup vegetable stock or broth or water
  • 3/4 cup fine-grind bulgur
  • 1 large tomato, seeded and diced
  • 1/2 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh flat-leaf (Italian) parsley
  • 3 tablespoons fresh chopped mint
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 large yellow onion
  • 1 table spoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan bring the vegetable stock to a boil.
  2. Place the bulgur in a large, heatproof bowl and pour in the boiling stock. Let stand until the bulgur is tender and the liquid is completely absorbed (about 15 minutes).
  3. Add all remaining ingredients and toss gently just until the ingredients are evenly distributed. Cover and refrigerate for two hours to allow the flavors to blend.
  4. Serve chilled.

Tabouleh – 1/2 Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3/8 cup vegetable stock or broth or water
  • 3/8 cup fine-grind bulgur
  • 1/2 large tomato, seeded and diced
  • 1/4 cucumber, peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1/8 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoons chopped fresh flat-leaf (Italian) parsley
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh chopped mint
  • 1 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/8 large yellow onion
  • 1/2 Tablespoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 3/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground allspice

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan bring the vegetable stock to a boil.
  2. Place the bulgur in a large, heatproof bowl and pour in the boiling stock.
  3. Let stand until the bulgur is tender and the liquid is completely absorbed, about 15 minutes.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients and toss gently just until the ingredients are evenly distributed. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours to allow the flavors to blend. Serve chilled.
