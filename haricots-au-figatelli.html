Haricots au Figatelli

Source: NoiTutti

Ingredients

  • 400 grams white beans
  • 1 slice of bacon (or a few slices of sausage, cut into pieces)
  • 1 (or 2) figatellu (I used kielbasa sausage)
  • (1 glass of red wine — I didn’t add this)
  • olive oil
  • 12 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bay leaf
  • thyme
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. After letting the beans soak overnight, wash and drain.
  2. With olive oil, fry the bacon, pieces of sausage, and chopped onion.
  3. When everything is golden, add tomato sauce, glass of wine, thyme, bay leaf, salt and pepper.
  4. Stir in beans, cover with water (or chicken or vegetable stock) and cook 1 hour over low heat.
  5. 16 oz white beans (Navy beans), let soak over night
  6. cook bacon
  7. cook onion in bacon grease and olive oil
  8. Add:
    • chopped bacon
    • slices of polska kielbasa (turkey, pork, and beef) or other sausage
    • 12 oz tomato sauce
    • thyme
    • herbes de provence
    • salt
    • pepper
  9. cover with chicken broth
  10. let simmer on low heat for one hour
