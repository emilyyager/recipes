Stir Fry

Ingredients – Sauce

  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1 tbs Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 1/3 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 heaping tbs Corn Starch
  • Some Crushed Red Pepper
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 2 tbs Minced Ginger

Ingredients – Vegetables

  • Chicken
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Broccoli
  • Red Bell Peppers

Tuna steak with soy sauce and vegetables: cherry tomatoes, lima beans, zucchini, snow peas, cilantro, sesame seeds, string beans (Guethary, France)

Directions

  1. Start boiling some rice or pasta
  2. Pan-fry chicken
  3. Mix up the ingredients for your sauce
  4. Saute the vegetables with the chicken, add when the chicken is still a little uncooked to keep from overcooking the chicken.
  5. Add the sauce to the vegetables & chicken. If the starch sticks to the pan, add some more water.
  6. Mix it with the rice or pasta and enjoy.
