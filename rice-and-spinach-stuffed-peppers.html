Rice- and Spinach-Stuffed Peppers

Source: Name, ___

Ingredients

  • 3 or 4 bell peppers, any color
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 large onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 jalapeno, chopped
  • 4.25 oz (1 can) green chilis
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 8 oz spinach
  • 1-inch piece of ginger
  • 6 oz plain greek yogurt
  • 1 bunch cilantro

Directions

  1. broil peppers 10 minutes, flip other side, broil 10 more minutes
  3. cook stuffing ingredients in a pan with olive oil
  4. last step: stuffed peppers, 350 degrees oven, 8 minutes
