Indian Potato Salad

Source: Albion Cooks

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of potatoes, cut into cubes
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 white onion or 3 scallions, chopped
  • 3 Tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons of cumin seeds
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • cayenne powder
  • 1/3 cup cilantro

Directions

  1. Boil the potatoes for 5 minutes; drain.
  2. In a bowl, combine onion and red pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil with cumin seeds.
  4. Add salt and curry powder.
  5. Pour over potato, onion, and red pepper mixture.
  6. Shake on cayenne.
  7. Add cilantro.
  8. Toss gently.
