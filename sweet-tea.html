Sweet Tea

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar (too sweet for Graham)
  • water
  • 2 tea bags

Directions

  1. Add sugar to percolator.
  2. Fill percolator with water, leaving room for water to boil.
  3. Boil sugar and water mixture. Sugar will dissolve as the water boils.
  4. Once water has boiled, remove from heat, add two tea bags, and then let steep for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir. Serve.
