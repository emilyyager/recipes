Cornbread Dressing

Ingredients

  • cornbread
  • 1 stick butter, maybe a little more
  • about 2 cups diced onion, it looks like a lot, but it will cook down. This is the amount for a single (not double) recipe of the cornbread dressing
  • about 2 cups diced celery
  • 1 14.5-ounce can of chicken broth (~2 cups)
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning, or use this recipe, substituting 1/4 teaspoon wherever you see 1 teaspoon, use the full amount made (about 2 teaspoons)
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Make cornbread. When cool, crumble into a bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan. Cook onion and celery.
  3. Pour butter, onion, and celery mixture over cornbread.
  4. Add chicken broth, poultry seasoning, egg, salt, and pepper. Stir. Pour mixture into cooking pan.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes, or until brown on top.
