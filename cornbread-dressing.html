Cornbread Dressing

Ingredients – Cornbread, using a mix in a box

  • cornmeal mix
  • 3 Tablespoons oil
  • 1 to 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten

Ingredients – Cornbread dressing

  • premade cornbread (see ingredients above) — 2 wheels
  • 1 stick butter, maybe a little more
  • onion — about 2 cups
  • celery — about 2 cups
  • one 14.5 ounce can of chicken broth (~2 cups)
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Make cornbread according to directions. When cool, crumble into a bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan. Cook onion and celery.
  3. Pour butter, onion, and celery mixture over cornbread.
  4. Add chicken broth, poultry seasoning, egg, salt, and pepper. Stir. Pour mixture into cooking pan.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes, or until brown on top.
