Pecan Pie

Source: My boyfriend's grandmother, Carolyn Rudd, got this recipe from a resort in Florida

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup white Karo syrup
  • 1 cup pecans, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 Tablespoons butter (half a stick)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • frozen pie crust

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Cream butter and sugar (soft butter best)
  3. Add salt, well beaten eggs, syrup and nuts.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Pour the filling into a frozen pie crust in a pie pan
  6. Bake for one hour or longer, until filling has firmed.
