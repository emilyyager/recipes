Peach Nectarine Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 peaches, peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 2 nectarines, peeled pitted, and diced
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 2 Tablespoons chopped fresh mint
  • 2 Tablespoons chopped fresh flatleaf (Italian) parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon grated orange zest
  • 2 Tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • 2 Tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Directions

  1. Combine all but the salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  2. Toss to mix.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
  4. Stir in salt and pepper just before serving. Serve at room temperature.
