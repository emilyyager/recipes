Croutons

Source: Savory Sweet Life

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bread (fresh or going stale)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil (
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley flakes
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
  2. Mix olive oil, parsley flakes, garlic powder, and salt until garlic powder is dissolved and mixture is clump free.
  3. In a medium bowl, toss olive oil mixture with bread cubes until evenly coated.
  4. Spread coated bread cubes on a cookie sheet in a single layer. Bake for 15 minutes. Check to see if they are dry, crispy, and golden brown.
  5. Stir the croutons around and bake for an additional 15 minutes until done.
  6. Store croutons in air-tight container or ziplock bag.
