Coleslaw

Source: Big Oven

Ingredients – Vegetables

  • 1 medium head of cabbage, washed, cored and shredded
  • 2 medium carrots peeled, grated
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, cored, seeded, diced fine
  • 2 Tablespoons white onion, grated

Ingredients – Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • less than 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup or 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • Optional: 2 Tablespoons celery seed

Directions

  1. Place cabbage, carrots, green pepper and onion in a large bowl. Set aside. In another bowl, mix together all of the remaining ingredients well, insuring that sugar and spices are completely dissolved. Pour mixture over cabbage mixture and toss well to combine all.
  2. Cover and refrigerate 3 to 4 hours before serving to permit flavors to meld.
