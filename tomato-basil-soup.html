Tomato Soup

Source: Diana Ball

Ingredients

  • 8 cups (16 to 20) tomatoes, peeled, cored, and chopped (or 8 cups canned whole tomatoes, crushed)
  • 8 cups (64 oz) tomato juice or mixture of tomato juice and vegetable or chicken stock
  • 12 to 14 fresh, washed basil leaves, plus more for garnish
  • 2 cups (1 pint) heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 pound (2 sticks) sweet, unsalted butter
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • crusty bread

Directions

  1. Combine tomatoes, juice (or juice-stock mixture) in saucepan.
  2. Simmer over medium-low heat for 30 minutes.
  3. Cool slightly, then place in blender or food processor and process with 12 to 14 basil leaves to puree. This will have to be done in small batches.
  4. Return to saucepan and add cream and butter; stir over low heat until cream and butter are incorporated.
  5. Garnish with basil leaves and serve with crusty bread.
