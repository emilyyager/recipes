Paneer Makhni

Source: Indian Food Forever

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer — I used one wedge of hard Ricotta cheese and one container of cubed tofu from Harris Teeter, dried by being wrapped in a napkin
  • 200 grams fresh cream — I used heavy cream
  • 200 grams tomato puree — on can 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons butter — I used 4
  • 2 tablespoon ground (dried kasuri methi) — 2 tablespoons may be too much, makes it taste bitter; 2nd time I put 1 tablespoon
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (cayenne pepper)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder — used Graham’s homemade garam masala
  • 2 to 3 green chilies — used one can of green chilies
  • salt to taste — I just put a small palmful … could use more

Directions

  1. Melt the butter in a pan, add slit green chilies, tomato puree, fenugreek leaves, salt, red chili powder and simmer for 4-5 minutes.
  2. Add the cream and garam masala powder and cook further for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the paneer pieces and cook for a minute. Serve hot on rice; garnish with coriander.

Notes

I had this at Café of India on Wisconsin Ave. in Washington, DC.
