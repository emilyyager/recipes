Cinnamon RollsIngredients

– Dough
  • 1 1/4ounce package dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup (~5 Tablespoons) margarine
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups flour

    Directions

    – Dough Prep Time: 1 hr Total Time: 1h15m For the rolls, dissolve the yeast in the warm milk in a large bowl. Add sugar, margarine, salt, eggs, and flour, mix well. Knead the dough into a large ball, using your hands dusted lightly with flour. Put in a bowl, cover and let rise in a warm place about 1 hour or until the dough has doubled in size. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

    Ingredients

    – Filling
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 Tablespoons cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup margarine, softened

    Directions

    – Filling To make filling, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.

    Directions

    – Adding Filling to Dough Roll the dough out on a lightly floured surface, until it is approx. 21 inches long by 16 inches wide. It should be approx. 1/4 thick. Spread the softened margarine over the surface of the dough, then sprinkle the brown sugar and cinnamon evenly over the surface. Working carefully, from the long edge, roll the dough down to the bottom edge. Cut the dough into 13/4 inch slices, and place in a lightly greased baking pan. Bake for 12 minutes or until light golden brown. [[[[
