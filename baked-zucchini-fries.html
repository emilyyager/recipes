Baked Zucchini Fries

Source: Meatless Monday

Ingredients

  • a little olive oil, for preparing the sheet pan
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons nonfat milk
  • 2–3 large zucchinis, cut into 4-inch sticks
  • 1 1/2 cups (panko) bread crumbs*
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, shredded
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Toast bread or bake in the oven.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  3. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil
  4. Spray a mist of olive oil on the baking sheet
  5. Lightly beat the eggs and milk together in a medium bowl.
  6. In a separate medium bowl, stir the panko breadcrumbs and cheese together.
  7. Dip zucchini sticks in the egg mixture, making sure to coat them well.
  8. Dip the egg coated zucchini into the panko mixture.
  9. Spread out the coated zucchini pieces onto the prepared sheet pan.
  10. Season generously with salt and pepper and bake for about 15 minutes.
  11. Flip the zucchini sticks over with a spatula and bake for another 15 minutes, or until they are golden brown.
