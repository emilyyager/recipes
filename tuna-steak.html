Tuna Steak Source: About.com

Ingredients

  • 2 1-1/2 inch to 2 inch thick tuna steaks
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • fresh coarsely ground black pepper
  • butter
  • 1 lemon, quartered

Directions

  1. Mix the spices and sprinkle over both sides of the tuna steaks.
  2. Heat stove at medium heat until butter sizzle when water is dripped in. Patience! It could take five minutes to heat up.
  3. Add one Tablespoon of butter to the pan.
  4. Place the steaks in the pan and slide them around to keep them from sticking. Cook the steaks for three minutes on the first side.
  5. Flip the steaks. Cook for 2 minutes on the second side
