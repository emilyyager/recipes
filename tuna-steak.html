Tuna Steak Source: About.com

Ingredients

  • 2 tuna steaks, 1-1/2 to 2 inches thick
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon butter
  • 1 lemon, quartered

Directions

  1. Mix the spices and spread over both sides of the tuna steaks. Grind black pepper on both sides as well.
  2. Heat stove at medium heat until butter sizzles when water is dripped in. (Be patient. It could take five minutes to heat up.)
  3. Add one Tablespoon of butter to the pan. Melt.
  4. Place the steaks in the pan and slide them around to keep them from sticking. Cook the steaks for three minutes on the first side.
  5. Flip the steaks. Cook for 2 minutes on the second side
  6. Serve with lemon slices for squeezing.
