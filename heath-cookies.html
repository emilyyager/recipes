Heath Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 sticks butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heath bits

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Cream the butter, white, and brown sugar.
  3. Mix in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Add flour, baking soad, salt, and heath bits.
  5. Place as balls on parchment paper on pan.
  6. Bake for 12 minutes.
