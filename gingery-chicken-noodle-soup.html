Gingery Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces of dried soba noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon ginger, peeled and minced
  • 1 carrot, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken stock or broth
  • 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, chopped
  • 1 cup shelled edamame
  • 1 cup plain soy milk
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped

Directions

  1. Bring a saucepan 3/4 full of water to a boil, add the noodles, and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Drain and set aside until needed.
  3. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add the onion and sauté until soft and translucent, about 4 minutes.
  5. Add the ginger and carrot and sauté for 1 minute.
  6. Add the garlic and sate for 30 second do not let the garlic brown.
  7. Add the stock and soy sauce and bring to a boil.
  8. Add the chicken and edamame and return to boil.
  9. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until the chicken is cooked and the edamame are tender, about 4 minutes.
  10. Add the soba noodles and soy milk and cook until heated through; do not let boil.
  11. Remove from heat and stir in cilantro. Ladle into warmed individual bowls and serve immediately.
