Southwest Salad Dressing

source Sara Hobbs, West Tawakoni, Texas, Taste of Home (April/May 2010, p. 32)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup lime juice (3 limes)
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 Tablespoons ground cumin
  • 1 Tablespoon salt
  • 1 Tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons coarsely ground pepper

Directions

  1. Mix it!
