"The Diabeetus" (Super Sweet)

Ingredients

  • 2 kiwis
  • 1 nectarine
  • 1 orange

Directions

  1. Slice the orange in half. Squeeze the juice of one half of the orange into the blender. Remove the pulp from the rind and add to the blender.
  2. Remove the rind from the other half of the orange and add the fruit to the blender. Remove any seeds that are easy to get to, but don't worry about the other seeds so much.
  3. Remove the peel from the two kiwis, cut the fruit into chunks, and add to the blender.
  4. Slice the nectarine in half. Remove the pit. Leave the peel on. Slice the fruit into chunks and add to the blender.
  5. Blend until smooth.

Notes

August 13, 2012
So sweet.
Color (from green and orange fruits) is gross!
Can see kiwi seeds and flecks of red nectarine peel. I like that.

Banana, Kiwi, Orange

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 kiwis
  • 2 oranges
  • 1 orange
  • 1 handful of ice cubes

Directions

  1. Slice one orange in half. Squeeze the juice of one half of the orange into the blender. Remove the pulp from the rind and add to the blender.
  2. Remove the rind from the other half of the orange and add the fruit to the blender. Remove any seeds that are easy to get to, but don't worry about the other seeds so much. Repeat for the second orange.
  3. Remove the peel from the two kiwis, cut the fruit into chunks, and add to the blender.
  4. Peal the banana and add to the blender.
  5. Blend until smooth.
  6. Add the handful of ice cubes.
  7. Again, blend until smooth.

Notes

August 16, 2012
Originally, with only one orange, the banana flavor was too strong, so I added a second orange. Now the kiwi is overwhelmed.
Color is a yellowish-green. Avocado green.
