Eggplant Rollatini

Adapted from Ellie Krieger, Accent Health
Yield: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • Olive oil spray
  • 1-1/2 pounds eggplant, trimmed and sliced lengthwise into 12 (1/4-inch thick) slices
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup lightly packed chopped fresh arugula
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated lemon zest
  • 1 glove garlic, minced
  • 12 large basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons finely grated fresh parmesan

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Line two baking trays with foil and coat each with olive oil spray.
  3. Arrange the eggplant in one layer and spray with oil.
  4. Season with 1/4 teaspoon each of the salt and pepper.
  5. Broil one tray at a time on high, about 5 inches from the flame, flipping the eggplant halfway through, until golden brown on both sides and tender, about 6 minutes total. Let cool.
  6. Spoon half of the tomato sauce into the bottom of a 9-inch square glass baking dish.
  7. Whisk together the ricotta, egg, arugula, lemon zest, and garlic, with the remaining salt and pepper.
  8. Arrange basil leaves on each slice of eggplant and top with about 2 tablespoons ricotta filling, spreading it evenly.
  9. Starting at the short end, roll up eggplant and arrange seam side down in the prepared baking dish.
  10. Spoon the remaining sauce over the eggplant rolls and sprinkle with parmesan.
  11. Bake uncovered in middle of oven, until sauce is bubbling and heated through, 25 to 30 minutes.
