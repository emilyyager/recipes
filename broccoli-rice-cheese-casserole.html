Broccoli Rice Casserole

Prep time: 30 min Cook time: 1 hour Total time: 1 hour 30 min Yield: 1 9" pie (8 servings)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry rice, cooked (yields 2 cups)
  • 1 small package of broccoli, chopped
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/8 cup oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pound American Velveeta cheese, diced
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Cook rice and broccoli.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over rice and broccoli mixture.
  4. Cook in a greased pan at 350 degrees for 1/2 hour covered, then 1/2 hour uncovered.
