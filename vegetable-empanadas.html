Vegetable Empanadas

Ingredients

  • onion and carrots
  • red peppers
  • squash (and/or zucchini)
  • spinach
  • dash of basil
  • dash of fresh chopped parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon poultry seasoning (marjoram, thyme)
  • cream cheese
  • egg yolk, if covering empanadas with egg wash
  • box of puff pastry(two sheets)
  • parchment paper

Directions

  1. Remove puff pastry from freezer.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  3. Sauté the vegetables in olive oil.
  4. Season with dash basil, dash parsley, 1/4 teaspoon poultry seasoning (marjoram, thyme).
  5. Cut each sheet of puff pastry into four squares.
  6. Place a spoonful of vegetables in the middle of each puff pastry square.
  7. Mash a dollop of cream cheese in with the vegetables.
  8. Wet two adjacent edges of the puff pastry to help the edges stick together.
  9. Fold the puff pastry squares in half into a triangle. Pinch edges closed.
  10. Spread water or egg wash over the tops of the puff pastry triangles
  11. Place triangles on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  12. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
