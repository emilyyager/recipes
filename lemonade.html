Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of sugar (or a little less)
  • 1 cup of water (to heat with the sugar)
  • 1 cup of lemon juice, squeezed from 4 to 6 lemons [used 5]
  • 5 cups of cool water

  • 1 cup of sugar (or a little less)
  • 1 1/3 cup water (to heat with the sugar)
  • 1 1/3 cup of lemon juice, squeezed from 4 to 6 lemons [used 6.5]
  • 6 2/3 cups of cool water

Directions

  1. Heat the sugar and 1 cup of water in a saucepan until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. While the sugar is dissolving, use a reamer to extract one cup of juice from 4 to 6 lemons.
  3. Combine the lemon juice and five cups of cool water. Allow the dissolved sugar-water (simple syrup) to cool before adding it to the pitcher. Mix. Let cool for 30 minutes before serving.
