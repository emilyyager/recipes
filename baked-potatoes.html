Baked Potato Source: Food Network

Ingredients

Olive oil: about 1 Tablespoon Sea salt 1/2 teaspoon potato 1
  • Sour cream, cheese, salt and pepper

    • Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350.
    2. Wash, poke holes, coat in oil.
    3. Put pan underneath in oven to catch drippings.
    4. Cook 45 min to 1 hr.
