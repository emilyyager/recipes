Oven-Roasted Beets

  • Adapted from: Vincent Scotto
  • Servings: 4 to 6

Ingredients

  • 6 medium beets
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 Tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 415 degrees.
  2. Remove beet greens and scrub beets. Peel off skin. Cut beets into bite-sized chunks.
  3. Wash, peel, and thinly slice onion.
  4. Combine beets and onion in a large bowl. Toss with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread beets and onion in a baking pan. Cover with aluminum foil, cutting a few slits to release steam.
  6. Roast beets in oven until tender, about one hour.
