Oven-Roasted Beets

Source: Chef Vincent Scotto, Scopa, New York Magazine
  • Servings: 4-6
  • Preparation Time: ___
  • Cooking Time: ___

Ingredients

  • 6 medium beets, 3 to 4 inches in diameter
  • 1 medium onion, sliced thin
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 415 degrees.
  2. With a paring knife, cut the ends off the beets and trim any roots. Scrub the beets, cut each beet in half, and cut each half into 3 equal pieces. Place the beets in a large bowl and toss with the onion, oil, and vinegar; then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Spread the beets and onion in a baking pan. Cover with aluminum foil. Cut a few slits in the foil to release the steam.
  4. Cook the beets in the oven for about one hour, or until beets are tender.
