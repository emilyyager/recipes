Cornbread

  • Adapted from a recipe by Mark Bittman.
  • Use this cornbread to make cornbread dressing

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter (canola oil, olive oil, lard, bacon drippings) for greasing pan
  • 1 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/4 cups milk (10 oz.)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Lightly grease baking pan(s) with butter.
  2. In a large bowl, mix:
    • cornmeal
    • flour
    • sugar
    • baking soda
    • baking powder
    • salt
  3. In a separate bowl, mix:
    • eggs
    • milk
  4. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry mixture and fold until it is no longer dry (batter will be lumpy).
  5. Pour the batter into the baking pan(s).
  6. Bake 20 to 25 minutes, until the top is golden and tester comes out clean.
  7. Let cornbread cool.
