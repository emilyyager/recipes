Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups buttermilk (12oz.)
  • 2 tablespoons butter OR canola oil for greasing pan

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Lightly grease baking pan(s) with butter.
  2. In a large bowl, mix:
    • cornmeal
    • flour
    • sugar
    • baking soda
    • baking powder
    • salt
  3. In a separate bowl, mix:
    • eggs
    • buttermilk
  4. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry mixture and fold until it is no longer dry (batter will be lumpy).
  5. Pour the batter into the baking pan(s).
  6. Bake 20 to 25 minutes, until the top is golden and tester comes out clean.
  7. Let cornbread cool.
This recipe is a cross between recipes by Alton Brown and Paula Deen.
