Spicy Asian Coleslaw Salad

Sources: Sanura Weathers and Cookin' Canuck

Ingredients – Dressing

  • 1 serrano or jalapeño chile pepper, seeded and membranes removed, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons peeled and grated fresh ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated lime zest
  • the juice of one lime (2–3 Tablespoons)
  • 2 Tablespoons canola oil
  • 3 Tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 to 2 tsp. of soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/8 to 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper, to taste
  • pepper to taste, salt if desired (already has soy sauce)

Ingredients – Dry Vegetables

  • 1 small Napa cabbage (about 1–1 1/2 pounds), cleaned, halved lengthwise, cored, and cut crosswise into 1/2-inch slices
  • 3 carrots; peeled and grated (1 1/2 cups)
  • 4 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together jalapeño, ginger, lime zest, lime juice, canola oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, crushed red pepper, and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Separate the cabbage leaves into a large bowl. Add carrots, green onions, and cilantro. Toss well.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cabbage mixture and toss again. Let stand for 10 minutes, tossing occasionally.
