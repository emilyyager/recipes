Carrot-Ginger-Tomato Soup

Source: Rozanne Gold, Eat Fresh Food: Awesome Recipes for Teen Chefs

Yield: 4 servings (5 cups)

Ingredients

  • 1 large bunch of fresh carrots, about 12 oz.
  • 1 large baking potato, about 8 oz.
  • 4 large garlic cloves, peeled and chopped
  • 2 Tablespoons fresh ginger, chopped
  • 1 small onion (2 large shallots), peeled and chopped
  • 2 small tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 3 Tablespoons unsalted butter

Directions

  1. Peel the carrots and the potato. Cut into 1-inch pieces and put in a stockpot (3-quart pot). Add garlic cloves, ginger, onion, and tomato. Add 4 cups of water and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Bring to a rapid boil. Lower the heat to medium and cover. Cook 30 minutes or until vegetables are very soft.
  2. Blend using immersion blender.
  3. Add the butter. Simmer for 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
