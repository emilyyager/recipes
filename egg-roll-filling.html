Egg Roll Insides

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 or 4 carrots, cut julienne style
  • 1 head of cabbage, cored and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Italian parsley

Directions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil in frying pan until onion is yellow, set aside in bowl.
  2. Sauté julienne carrots in olive oil about 3 minutes, set aside in bowl. Sauté cabbage in olive oil, until cabbage is just beginning to wilt, about 3 minutes. Set aside in bowl.
  3. Mix together rice vinegar, soy sauce, honey, salt, pepper, crushed red pepper, and Italian parsley. Pour over cabbage mixture in bowl. Toss.

For added spiciness:

  • 1/2 Serrano pepper, seeded and de-membraned, finely chopped
  • jalapeno pepper

Could also add:

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon peeled and grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated lime zest
  • Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons sugar (instead of honey)
