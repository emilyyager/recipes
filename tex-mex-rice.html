Tex-Mex Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup uncooked long grain rice
  • (1 teaspoon garlic salt) I didn’t put this last time
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin (I used cumin seeds)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion – 1/2 medium onion
  • 4 oz. (1/2 cup) tomato sauce
  • 1 14.5oz. can of chicken broth
  • Carrots (optional)
  • Peas (optional)

Directions

  1. Rinse rice in a colander until the water runs clear.
  2. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat and add rice. Cook, stirring constantly, until rice is puffed and golden. While rice is cooking, sprinkle with garlic salt and cumin.
  3. Stir in onion and cook until tender. Stir in tomato sauce and broth. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
