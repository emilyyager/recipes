Spicy Roasted Chickpeas and Carrots with Lime

Source: So Good & Tastyserves 4 as a side

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 small bunch carrots, roughly chopped
  • 3 cups cooked chickpeas or about 2 15-ounce cans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • juice of one lime
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400˚F.
  2. Place carrots, chickpeas, olive oil, and spices into a bowl, sprinkle with salt and toss everything to coat evenly.
  3. Using half the lime, squeeze juice over the bowl and give everything one more quick stir.
  4. Transfer everything to a rimmed baking sheet and roast until the carrots and chickpeas are lightly browned, about 25 minutes. Make sure you stir occasionally for even cooking.
  5. Once out of the oven, squeeze on more lime juice, if desired, and adjust the salt if need be.
  6. Serve warm. Can be made ahead and re-warmed in the oven at 400˚F for about 5 minutes.
