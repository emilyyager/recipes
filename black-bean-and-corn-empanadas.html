Black Bean and Corn Empanadas

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, drained, but not rinsed
  • 1 can corn, drained, but not rinsed
  • 1/2 small onion, diced, sautéed
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • cilantro, chopped
  • jalapeño, chopped
  • shredded cheddar jack cheese
  • salt
  • ground cumin
  • garlic powder
  • egg yolk, if covering empanadas with an egg wash
  • box of puff pastry(two sheets)
  • parchment paper

Directions

  1. Remove puff pastry from freezer.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  3. Sauté the onion in olive oil.
  4. Add remaining vegetables.
  5. Season with dash salt, dash ground cumin, dash garlic powder.
  6. Cut each sheet of puff pastry into four squares.
  7. Place a spoonful of vegetables in the middle of each puff pastry square.
  8. Sprinkle on shredded cheese over the vegetables.
  9. Wet two adjacent edges of the puff pastry to help the edges stick together.
  10. Fold the puff pastry squares in half into a triangle. Pinch edges closed.
  11. Spread water or egg wash over the tops of the puff pastry triangles
  12. Place triangles on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  13. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
