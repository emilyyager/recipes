Salsa (Casera/Ranchera Style)

Source: Red Salsa, Green Salsa

Ingredients

  • 6–9 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 2–3 jalapeno peppers
  • 1/2 of a large yellow onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • salt
  • small amount of black pepper
  • cilantro
  • one lime or lime juice
  • other peppers: Anaheim, serrano

Directions

  1. poke holes in tomatoes (cut off top part around stem), score skin to make it easier to peel later; and jalapenos and broil for 30 minutes;
  2. Dice/crush garlic in a bowl
  3. Dice onion and add to bowl
  4. Peel tomatoes and mash up in bowl
  5. Dice jalapeno peppers Add juice of 1/3 lime
  6. Add salt and pepper and cilantro
  7. Squeeze in lime juice
