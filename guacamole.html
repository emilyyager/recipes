Guacamole

Source: Alton Brown, Food Network

Ingredients

  • 2 small tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 3 Haas avocados, halved, seeded and peeled
  • 1 lime, juiced

Directions

  1. Dice the tIn a large bowl—preferably glass—
  2. In a large bowl place the scooped avocado pulp and lime juice, toss to coat.
  3. Drain, and reserve the lime juice, after all of the avocados have been coated.
  4. Using a potato masher add the salt, cumin, and cayenne and mash. Then, fold in the onions, tomatoes, cilantro, and garlic.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of the reserved lime juice.
  6. Let sit at room temperature for 1 hour.
  7. Serve.
