Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 sticks butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 2 cups (12 oz) semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Bake for 13 to 15 minutes.
