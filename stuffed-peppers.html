Stuffed Peppers (Chiles Rellenos)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Salt
  • Fajita seasoning
  • Cumin
  • Garlic
  • 1/2 of a medium-sized onion
  • 4 green bell peppers, Anaheim peppers, or poblano peppers
  • Sargento Mexican cheese mix

Directions

  1. Heavy skillet
  2. Medium heat
