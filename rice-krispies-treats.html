Rice Krispies Treats

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons of margarine or butter
  • one 10ounce package of regular marshmallows (or 4 cups of mini-marshmallows)
  • 6 cups of Rice Krispies

Directions

  1. Melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until melted.
  2. Add Rice Krispies and stir until coated.
  3. Pour the mixture into a pan and let cool. When cool, cut into squares and serve.
