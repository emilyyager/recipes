Pancakes

Source: The Joy of Cooking, Link

Dry 

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Wet 

Ingredients

  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Directions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together, then add wet ingredients.
