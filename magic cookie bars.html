Magic Cookie Bars

Ingredients

:
  • 1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker, crushed
  • 1 cup nuts (walnuts)
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 1/3 cups flaked coconut
  • 1 1/3 cups sweetened condensed milk (1 can)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
  2. Melt butter in a 11”×13” pan.
  3. Layer ingredients in the order listed.
  4. Bake for 25 minutes.
  5. Allow bars to cool before cutting into squares.
