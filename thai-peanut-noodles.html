Thai Peanut Noodles

Source: ___, Cafe Johnsonia

Cook time

Yield:

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a package of rice noodles
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3-4 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3-4 Tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/4-1/2 teaspoons Sriracha
  • (1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped finely)I have never added this
  • (1 cooked chicken breast, shredded)
  • cilantro
  • grated carrots (julienned if you're fancy)

Directions

  1. Prepare noodles according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup cooking water and drain noodles. (Use salted water.) Place in a large serving bowl and keep warm.
  2. While the noodles are cooking, place peanut butter and brown sugar in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Place in the microwave for about 30 seconds--just long enough to melt it slightly. Add the remaining ingredients and stir to mix well. Taste and adjust seasoning to taste. Toss the noodles with the peanut sauce and add the pasta water, if necessary, so the sauce evenly coats the noodles. Add the shredded chicken, if using, and sprinkle with peanuts.
